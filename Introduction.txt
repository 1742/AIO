[h2]Core Gameplay: Law Editing (DAIT)[/h2]
Just like the three vanilla Crisis paths, you need to adopt three Ascension Perks before you can select [b]All-in-One[/b]. By accumulating [b]Transcendental Logic[/b], you are no longer simply researching technologies, but directly converting logic into empire modifiers.
The current Crisis Buffs are divided into three categories. You can switch between them at any time.

[h2]Walkthrough[/h2]

[h3]Crisis Level 1[/h3]
Unlocked upon adoption:
[list]
[*][b]Exponential Learning Theory:[/b] Unlocks the Recursive Node District. Infinite build limit, produces Research and Unity.
[*][b]Horizon Engine:[/b] Produces Energy and Dark Matter.
[/list]

[h3]Crisis Level 2[/h3]
Unlocked after completing the [i][b]Horizon Engine[/b][/i]:
[list]
[*]Upgrade to the [i][b]Gate of All Gates[/b][/i]. Note: High Energy upkeep, especially during the [b]Fusion Universe[/b] Situation.
[*][b]Strategy:[/b] Stockpile Energy before upgrading. Once the Situation starts, select [b]Vacuum Zero-Point Level[/b] in DAIT to prevent an economic crash.
[/list]

[b]Fusion Universe Situation[/b]
Spend massive Energy and Dark Matter to advance.
[list]
[*]Stage 1: Unlocks [b]Gate of All Gates Node[/b] (Gateway).
[*]Stage 2: Unlocks special system [i][b]The Nameless Mist[/b][/i].
[/list]

[h3]Crisis Level 3-5[/h3]
Upgrade to [i][b]Silver Key[/b][/i] -> [i][b]Eternal Throne[/b][/i] -> [i][b]Yog-Sothoth[/b][/i].
Completing Stage 7 grants victory. You can continue playing to edit reality (delete stars, etc.) using Yog-Sothoth.
